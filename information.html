3.How Long Will It Take For Delivery?

Total delivery time is split into two parts.
a. Processing time 3-7 business days normally: The time from when your payment is received to when we gather up all the items, do strict quality-control tests, and carefully package the items.
b. Shipping time 5-25 business days: This is the time that it takes for the package to be delivered by the couriers to the destination, Here shows delivery times for every shipping method.

4. Will I have to pay custom tax?

Tax is charged by Customs offices in destination country based on their rules and policies. The total price of any order will only include the applicable sales taxes. All other taxes (e.g:Import Tax) that may apply are the sole responsibility of and to be paid by the customer. Please note that according to customs regulations, Alluregal cannot declare purchased items as a gift or list an amount that lower than the actual product price.
